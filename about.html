About Me

I am extremely excited about attending Rutgers Coding Bootcamp. I have devoted half of my year to learning full-stack JavaScript development, which will allow me to program server-side actions as well as client-side interaction.

We've been learning at a very fast pace, but I have a handle on BootStrap, as shown on this portfolio. I've conformed a previously made HTML layout to a BootStrap theme. After this, I will be onto learning the basics of JavaScript. How exciting!
